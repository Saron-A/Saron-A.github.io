About Me

I am Saron Abebe, a passionate and dedicated front-end developer with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, I continuously seek opportunities to deepen my knowledge and refine my skills. My learning journey extends beyond the classroom—I am actively engaged in self-paced learning through The Odin Project, where I explore full-stack web development. I also hold a certificate in coding and web development from SheCodes, which has strengthened my understanding of modern web technologies and best practices. My approach to development emphasizes clean, responsive design and intuitive user experiences. Whether I'm building portfolio websites, mobile apps, or functional web tools, I strive to combine creativity with logical problem-solving. I enjoy working on projects that have real-world impact and reflect a blend of cultural identity, accessibility, and user-centered design. I am currently seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects and further expand my expertise in web and mobile development.
